## Summary

*Who Cares For The Unborn Baby?* is a Christian pro-life devotional work focused on the sanctity of human life, especially the life of the unborn child. Ron G. Christian argues that human life has sacred value because people are created in the image of God, loved by their Creator, and redeemed through Jesus Christ. The book presents a biblical foundation for human dignity through creation, the incarnation of Christ, Jesus' compassion for the vulnerable, His atoning death, stewardship of the body, the law of love, the Golden Rule, human accountability before God, and the bodily resurrection. It strongly condemns abortion as a grave moral wrong while also emphasizing that God's grace, forgiveness, repentance, and reconciliation are available to sinners through the blood of Jesus. The book also broadens the sanctity-of-life theme beyond abortion to include care for the weak, the needy, the suffering, and all people whose lives bear eternal worth before God.
