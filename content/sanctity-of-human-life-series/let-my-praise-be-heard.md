**Title:** Let My Praise Be Heard!

**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Let My Praise Be Heard!* is a devotional Christian work focused on gratitude, praise, thanksgiving, and trust in God through both joyful and painful circumstances. Ron G. Christian argues that believers are called to give thanks *in* all things, not necessarily *for* all things, because God is loving, wise, powerful, and able to transform even hardship into spiritual growth. The book contrasts gratitude with ingratitude, pride, bitterness, and self-sufficiency, presenting thankfulness as a mark of humility and holiness. Through personal stories about family members, friends, suffering, rescue, healing, ministry, creation, and soul-winning opportunities, the author shows how praise can become a way of life. The book emphasizes that gratitude is not merely a human achievement but a gift and practice empowered by the Holy Spirit, helping believers maintain faith, balance, joy, and hope as they look toward eternal life in heaven.
