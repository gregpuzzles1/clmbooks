**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Let My Laughter Resound!* is a Christian devotional work by Ron Christian about the spiritual value of joy, wholesome laughter, humility, and self-forgetfulness in the life of a believer. The book teaches that true laughter flows from the "joy of the Lord" and from a healthy relationship with God, oneself, and others. Christian proposes that believers should take God seriously while learning not to take themselves too seriously, allowing them to laugh at their own weaknesses, oddities, and embarrassing moments without defensiveness or pride. The book reflects on Jesus as both sorrowful and joyful, suggesting that Christ's victory over sin, death, and Satan gives believers deep reason for "holy laughter." Through personal stories, humorous memories, marriage reflections, and devotional teaching, the book encourages readers to embrace humility, transparency, joy, and loving relationships as gifts from God.
