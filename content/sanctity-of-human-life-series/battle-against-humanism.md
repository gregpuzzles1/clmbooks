**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Battle Against Humanism!* is a Christian devotional and apologetic work by Ron G. Christian that contrasts a Christ-centered worldview with what the author describes as humanism: a self-centered, prideful, materialistic, and God-denying philosophy. The book argues that science and human reason are valuable gifts from God, but become spiritually dangerous when elevated into objects of worship or treated as the only path to truth. Christian emphasizes that both Scripture and nature reveal God, and he presents the ordered universe as evidence of divine design. Throughout the book, he calls believers to humility, prayer, obedience, compassion, and dependence on the Holy Spirit rather than self-sufficiency. The work also addresses related themes such as relativism, materialism, abortion, public moral standards, the teaching of the Bible, spiritual gifts, Christian service, heaven, suffering, praise, tears, laughter, and God’s love. Its central message is that the Christian life is not built on human pride or independence, but on repentance, faith in Christ, humble service, and loving witness to others.
