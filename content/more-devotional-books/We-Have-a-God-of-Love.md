**PDF Title:** *We Have a God of Love!*

**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*We Have a God of Love!* is a wide-ranging Christian devotional work by Ron Christian that presents God’s love as the central reality revealed through Jesus Christ, the cross, the resurrection, Scripture, prayer, and daily discipleship. The book maintains that Jesus is the incomparable Son of God - Wonderful Counselor, Mighty God, Everlasting Father, and Prince of Peace - whose incarnation and sacrifice show both the costliness of divine love and the depth of God’s desire to redeem humanity. Christian asserts that the Gospel is not merely a religious idea, but the greatest love story: God takes the initiative, offers salvation to all, calls each person to repentance and faith, and gives assurance of eternal life through Christ. Across its chapters, the book proposes that Christian living should be shaped by gratitude, humility, prayer, Scripture, compassion, stewardship, unity, and perseverance amid temptation, suffering, fear, and unanswered questions. It also contends that Christ’s resurrection secures hope beyond death and that God’s loving presence remains near to believers on life’s lonely road.
