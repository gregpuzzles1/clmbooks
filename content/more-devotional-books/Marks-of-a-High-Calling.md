**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Marks of a High Calling* is a Christian devotional and theological work by Ron Christian that explores the profound impact of a Christ-centered life and outlines the spiritual journey toward finding and establishing a believer's calling. The book outlines how the Incarnation of Jesus transforms all human relationships and exalts daily human existence. Through a detailed analysis of biblical parables and characters—such as the prodigal son, the self-righteous Pharisee, and the repentant tax collector—Christian contends that true faith requires deep humility, repentance, and a total reliance on divine grace rather than human self-sufficiency. The work explores the pitfalls of secularism and materialism, illustrating how modern cultural standards fail to satisfy the human spirit. Furthermore, it details how a deep relationship with God delivers individuals from terrorizing fears, bestows genuine personal significance, and motivates a dedicated life of spiritual discipline, witnessing, and humble service.
