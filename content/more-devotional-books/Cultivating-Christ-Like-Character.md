**PDF Title:** *Cultivating Christlike Character*

**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Cultivating Christlike Character* is an in-depth devotional study by Ron Christian on the Spirit-filled life and the formation of Christian character. The book maintains that believers grow in Christlikeness not by human effort alone, but through surrender to Christ, the cleansing and empowering work of the Holy Spirit, and daily cooperation with God’s grace. Christian presents the Holy Spirit as the source of conviction, cleansing, comfort, guidance, power, and moral transformation, calling believers to move beyond self-centeredness, fear, division, and spiritual defeat. The work also proposes that genuine Christian maturity is seen in practical holiness: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control. Through devotional readings, Scripture references, prayers, affirmations, and discussion questions, the book encourages readers to cultivate a tender heart, renewed mind, surrendered will, active hands, and a Christ-centered life that bears the fruit of the Spirit in everyday relationships and service.
