## Summary

*And Jesus Taught Them Saying...* is a devotional study by Ron Christian centered on Jesus’ Sermon on the Mount and several of His life-changing parables. The work presents Jesus as both conservative and revolutionary: one who preserves the original biblical message of love while calling His followers into transformed, practical, and contemporary obedience. Christian maintains that the Sermon on the Mount offers a pattern for victorious Christian living through humility, mourning over sin, meekness, hunger for righteousness, mercy, purity of heart, peacemaking, faithful endurance, prayer, forgiveness, and obedience. The book also explores Jesus’ teachings on law, inward purity, marriage and divorce, communication, self-denial, love, judgment, treasure, false prophets, and the narrow way. Its parable studies emphasize spiritual readiness, genuine religion, the cost of discipleship, the growth of God’s kingdom, God’s equal rewards, judgment, preparedness, and persistent prayer. Overall, the book calls believers to let Christ’s teachings shape their motives, relationships, priorities, and daily conduct.
