**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*How to Obtain and Maintain Eternal Salvation* is a Christian devotional study by Ron Christian that explores both how a person receives salvation through Christ and how believers continue faithfully in eternal life. The book maintains that salvation is grounded in the atoning work of Jesus Christ, received through repentance, faith, surrender, and personal trust rather than human merit or religious self-effort. Ron Christian addresses the long-standing debate over eternal security, while emphasizing shared Christian convictions about Christ as the only Savior, the reality of heaven and hell, the authority of Scripture, and the necessity of God’s grace. The work contends that people must honestly face sin, guilt, indifference, shallow responses, worldly distractions, and fears of unworthiness, then respond to Christ with repentance, obedient faith, and continuing dependence on the Holy Spirit. It also proposes that eternal life is not merely a future reward, but a present relationship with God that must be cultivated through prayer, obedience, holiness, perseverance, and abiding in Christ. Its central message is that God freely offers salvation to all, but each person must receive, cherish, and continue in that saving relationship with Christ.
