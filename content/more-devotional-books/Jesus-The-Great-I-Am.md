**PDF Title:** *Jesus - The Great 'I Am'!*

**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Jesus - The Great 'I Am'!* is a devotional and Bible-study work by Ron Christian that explores the distinctive titles and self-revelations of Jesus in the Gospel of John. The book maintains that Jesus is the eternal Word, the true Light, the source of life, the object of true worship, the way to dynamic faith, the bread of life, the Good Shepherd, the resurrection, the way, the truth, and the life, the vine, and the faithful protector of His people. Christian contends that each title reveals both the character of Christ and the practical needs of humanity, showing Jesus as the One who gives spiritual illumination, forgiveness, joy, healing, guidance, nourishment, eternal life, and abiding fruitfulness. The work asserts that genuine Christian living requires personal surrender to Christ, continued trust in His Word, prayerful dependence, obedient faith, and a willingness to let His life reshape the believer's mind, heart, relationships, worship, and service. Through outlines, Scripture readings, devotional reflections, and discussion questions, the book proposes that the Gospel of John presents Jesus not merely as a teacher or religious figure, but as the divine Son of God who fully reveals the Father and invites every person into saving, transforming fellowship with Him.
