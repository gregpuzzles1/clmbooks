**PDF Title:** *Does the New Testament Teach - "Conditional Eternal Security"?*

**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Does the New Testament Teach - "Conditional Eternal Security"?* is a Christian devotional and theological study by Ron Christian that examines perseverance, apostasy, faithfulness, and the believer’s continuing relationship with God. The work maintains that Scripture contains both strong promises of God’s keeping power and serious warnings that believers must continue in faith, obedience, repentance, and love. Christian contends that salvation is not maintained by human strength, but by abiding in Christ, depending on the Holy Spirit, and responding faithfully to God’s grace. The study contrasts unconditional eternal security with what it presents as a conditional New Testament pattern: God is faithful to preserve believers, yet believers are called to persevere, resist spiritual drift, avoid deliberate sin, and keep trusting Christ. Drawing especially from Hebrews and many other New Testament passages, the work emphasizes that God’s promises, conditions, and warnings belong together. Its central message is that Christian assurance is real and deeply comforting, but it is inseparable from a living, obedient, and persevering faith in Christ.
