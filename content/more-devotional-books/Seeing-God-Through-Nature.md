**PDF Title:** *Seeing God Through Nature!*

**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Seeing God Through Nature!* is a Christian devotional reflection by Ron Christian that maintains God reveals aspects of His character through the created world. Beginning with a personal account of an early-morning jog, Ron Christian proposes that nature points beyond itself to the beauty, order, power, magnitude, and variety of the Creator. The paper especially develops the theme of God's power, asserting that nature displays divine strength while also raising questions about evil, natural disaster, human freedom, and God's sovereign yet self-restrained relationship with mankind. Ron Christian contends that God created human beings as free moral agents, able to choose love, obedience, repentance, and faith rather than being controlled like machines. The work also reflects on God's perfect will and permissive will, the role of Satan and fallen creation, the Holy Spirit's gracious influence, and the need for people to respond personally to God's revelation. Its central message is that nature is not an impersonal accident but a witness to a beautiful, powerful, loving Creator who seeks to draw people into repentance, faith, holiness, and loving fellowship with Himself.
