**Author:** Ron Christian, Writer/Compiler

## Summary

*Conversion and Consummation* is a broad Christian devotional compilation by Ron Christian that explores God's plan of salvation from the first awakening of faith through final glorification in Christ. The work maintains that God reveals Himself progressively through nature, conscience, Scripture, history, and most fully through Jesus Christ, the eternal Word and complete Light of God. It contends that salvation is grounded in God's universal love, Christ's atoning death, repentance, faith, forgiveness, justification, regeneration, and the transforming work of the Holy Spirit. Ron Christian also emphasizes that human beings are fallen and responsible moral creatures, yet every person is loved by God and invited to receive grace rather than remain bound by sin, guilt, fear, or spiritual darkness. The book addresses topics such as prevenient grace, free will, assurance, sanctification, holiness, Christian service, suffering, death, resurrection, heaven, judgment, and eternal life. Its central message is that God's redemptive purpose is not merely to forgive sinners, but to transform them into Christlike people and finally bring them into the fullness of eternal glory with Him.
