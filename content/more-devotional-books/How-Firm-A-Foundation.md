**PDF Title:** *How Firm a Foundation!*

**Author:** Ronald G. Christian

## Summary

*How Firm a Foundation!* is an in-depth devotional study of the basic truths of Christianity by Ron Christian. The book maintains that Christ is the firm foundation for faith, meaning, moral responsibility, forgiveness, hope, and daily Christian living. Christian presents a broad survey of foundational doctrines, including humanity’s search for meaning, obstacles and incentives to faith, belief in a personal God, creation and redemption, the lost greatness of mankind, the identity of Christ, the incarnation, the sacrifice of God’s love, the redemption of lost humanity, the resurrection, and the reign of Christ. The work contends that human substitutes such as materialism, pleasure, popularity, power, and knowledge cannot satisfy the soul’s deepest longings. Instead, it proposes that lasting purpose, wisdom, moral strength, and joy are found through repentance, faith, surrender, and a personal relationship with the living Christ. Throughout the devotional, Ron Christian calls readers to thoughtful belief, honest self-examination, humility before God, and loving fellowship with others through Christ.
