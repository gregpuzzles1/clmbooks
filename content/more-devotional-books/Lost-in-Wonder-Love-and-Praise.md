**PDF Title:** *Lost in Wonder, Love and Praise*

**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Lost in Wonder, Love and Praise* is a devotional study by Ron Christian centered on selected Psalms and the believer’s call to become absorbed in God’s greatness, love, truth, and saving power. The book maintains that spiritual stability begins when a person turns away from worldly counsel, sinful paths, and cynical influences, and instead delights in God’s Word, trusts His promises, and lives under His revealed truth. Christian contends that wonder, love, and praise are not merely emotional responses, but a way of life that reshapes the mind, strengthens faith, deepens worship, and helps believers face discouragement, temptation, suffering, guilt, and fear. Through reflections on God’s majesty, creation, Scripture, providence, mercy, forgiveness, and protection, the book proposes that Christians can move from spiritual drifting and anxiety into confidence, obedience, joy, and fruitful service. Its central message is that the life of faith is sustained by beholding God’s character, receiving His grace, and learning to praise Him in every circumstance.
