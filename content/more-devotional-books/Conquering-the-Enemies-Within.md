**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Conquering the Enemies Within* is a topical Christian devotional study by Ron Christian that presents Christ as the answer to the inward struggles that keep believers from living the abundant Christian life. The book maintains that salvation is not limited to forgiveness of outward sins, but also includes deliverance from inward enemies such as real guilt, false guilt, fear, inferiority, jealousy, judgmentalism, negativism, worry, greed, destructive doubt, carnal anger, resentment, and self-centeredness. Christian contends that these struggles are conquered through honest self-examination, repentance, prayer, faith, humility, trust in God's love, and a willingness to receive God-wrought change. Each chapter combines Scripture, devotional reflection, practical counsel, prayers, affirmations, and discussion questions, making the book suitable for personal devotional reading or small-group study. Its central message proposes that believers can face their inner battles without despair because Christ offers forgiveness, cleansing, courage, emotional freedom, and power for a transformed life.
