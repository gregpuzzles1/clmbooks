**PDF Title:** *Let Us Hold Fast*

**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Let Us Hold Fast* is an in-depth devotional study of the book of Hebrews by Ron Christian that calls believers to remain steadfast in faith, hope, and obedience because Jesus Christ is supreme, compassionate, faithful, and sufficient. The book maintains that Hebrews presents Christ as greater than prophets, angels, Moses, priests, sacrifices, and every former means of approaching God, while also warning Christians against spiritual drifting, unbelief, discouragement, and careless neglect of grace. Christian emphasizes the believer’s need to hold firmly to confession, pursue maturity, rest in God’s promises, persevere through discipline, and cultivate Christlike character through faith and obedience. The work also explores the faith of biblical figures such as Noah, Abraham, and Moses, showing how their trust in God provides a model for Christian endurance. Its central message is that believers can continue faithfully because the promised Savior is faithful, merciful, victorious, and able to bring His people to spiritual maturity.
