**PDF Title:** *How Wide Is God's Mercy?*

**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*How Wide Is God's Mercy?* is a theological and devotional study by Ron Christian that examines predestination, the destiny of the unevangelized, the wideness of God's mercy, and the biblical basis for salvation. The book maintains that God's saving love extends to every person and carefully contrasts Calvinistic predestination with an Arminian understanding of human freedom, prevenient grace, and universal opportunity to respond to God. Ron Christian contends that God reveals Himself through creation, conscience, history, Scripture, and supremely through Jesus Christ, making every person accountable according to the light they have received. The work also proposes that God judges with perfect justice and mercy, taking into account each person's response to divine revelation rather than mere religious labels or outward affiliation. Throughout the study, numerous historical theologians and biblical passages are examined to support the conviction that God's mercy is far wider than many believers assume while still affirming Christ as the only Savior. Its central message is that God's character is perfectly loving, just, and merciful, and that He sincerely desires the salvation of all people.
