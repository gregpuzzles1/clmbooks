**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Happy Healthy Home!* is a Christian devotional guide by Ron Christian that presents the home as a place where faith, love, discipline, prayer, and service are to be practiced daily. The book maintains that godly family life begins with parents who put God first, model humility, cultivate wisdom, and teach children through both instruction and example. Christian emphasizes the responsibilities of mothers and fathers, including hospitality, simple living, faithful marriage, wise discipline, unconditional love, stewardship of money, regular prayer, Bible reading, and involvement in the local church. He contends that children should be trained to honor God, respect authority, resist temptation, value Scripture above worldly success, and develop their God-given gifts for service. The work also addresses practical moral concerns such as materialism, pride, sports idolatry, lying, sexual purity, peer pressure, decision-making, and the need to stand alone for righteousness when necessary. Its central message is that a truly happy and healthy home is built not merely on outward order or family affection, but on Christ-centered love, Spirit-filled character, Biblical wisdom, and a shared commitment to serve God and others.
