**PDF Title:** *Becoming a Mature Christian*

**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Becoming a Mature Christian* is a devotional Bible study by Ron Christian based on 1 Thessalonians and focused on the practical growth of Christian character, holiness, faith, hope, love, encouragement, and sanctification. The book proposes that spiritual maturity begins with receiving and proclaiming the Gospel, then grows through disciplined love, moral purity, diligent work, mutual encouragement, and steadfast hope in Christ’s return. Christian presents the mature believer as one whose faith is active, whose love overflows toward fellow believers and outsiders, and whose life displays integrity in ordinary responsibilities. The study also emphasizes the marks of spiritual leadership, the ministry of encouragement, the life of holiness, hope in an age of despair, Christian attitudes toward death and Christ’s coming, and the importance of being a mature church member. Its central message is that Christian maturity is not abstract or merely doctrinal, but practical, relational, holy, hopeful, and empowered by God’s grace through the Holy Spirit.
