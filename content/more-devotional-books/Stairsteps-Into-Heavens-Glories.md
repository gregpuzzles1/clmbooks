**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Stair Steps Into Heaven's Glories!* is a Christian devotional compilation by Ron Christian that reflects on heaven, death, resurrection hope, and Christian victory in dying. Drawing on Scripture, testimonies, devotional excerpts, and quotations from Christian writers, the book maintains that death is not the final enemy for believers but a passage into the presence of Christ. Ron Christian presents heaven as a prepared home marked by fellowship, beauty, rest, joy, worship, meaningful service, and reunion with God's people, while grounding that hope in Christ's resurrection rather than sentiment alone. The work also shares testimonies and reflections about faithful dying to encourage readers to face mortality with confidence, repentance, and trust in God's mercy. Across its sections, the book asserts that Christian hope does not deny grief, but answers grief with the assurance that Jesus has conquered death and promises eternal life. Its central message is that believers can move through suffering and death with peace because Christ prepares a place for His followers and will raise them to everlasting glory.
