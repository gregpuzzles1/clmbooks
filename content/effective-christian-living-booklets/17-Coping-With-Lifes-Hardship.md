**PDF Title:** *Coping With Life's Hardship!*

**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Coping With Life's Hardship!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that believers can face suffering, grief, loss, persecution, temptation, fear, and life’s troubling circumstances with hope because God remains present, faithful, and redemptively active. The booklet asserts that Christians are not promised immunity from hardship, but they are offered peace, strength, comfort, purpose, and spiritual security through trust in Christ. Ron Christian contends that adversity can become a means of spiritual growth, deeper dependence on God, greater compassion for others, and more fruitful Christian service when it is surrendered to God’s transforming grace. The work also proposes that not all suffering should be viewed as directly sent by God, yet God can still redeem brokenness, use trials to mature character, and turn tragedy into triumph. Its central message is that the cross of Christ, the care of the Heavenly Father, and the sustaining power of the Holy Spirit enable believers to endure life’s storms with courage, forgiveness, hope, compassion, and lasting confidence in God.
