## Summary

*Christians Witness To Others!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that every believer has both the responsibility and the opportunity to influence others for Christ. The booklet asserts that one person can make a meaningful difference in the world when surrendered to God, especially through prayer, compassionate service, godly example, and positive influence. Ron Christian contends that the Great Commission is not optional, but a mandate for all Christians to become disciple-makers through the power and love of the Holy Spirit. The work also proposes that effective witnessing must remain Christ-centered, because Jesus’ life, death, resurrection, and transforming grace are the true focus of salvation history. Its central message is that Christians are called to be sincere, Spirit-led witnesses who share the Good News with humility, love, patience, and confidence while leaving the results with Christ.
