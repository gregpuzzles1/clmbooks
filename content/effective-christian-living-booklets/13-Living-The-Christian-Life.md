## Summary

*Living The Christian Life!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that the Christian life is a daily walk of faith, wisdom, grace, repentance, spiritual growth, and practical obedience to God. The booklet asserts that materialism and accumulated wealth cannot satisfy the soul, while God-given wisdom, reverence for God, and trust in Christ provide guidance, protection, discernment, and true riches. Ron Christian contends that problems, suffering, injustices, failures, and conflicts need not defeat believers, because God can use trials for growth, comfort, ministry, maturity, and renewed dependence on His grace. The work also proposes that Christians must live under the dominion of grace, use their words for righteousness, cooperate with Christ, seek happiness through holiness, respond humbly to moral failure, and develop discernment amid life’s complexities. Its central message is that living the Christian life means trusting God’s promises, resisting sin, growing through difficulties, practicing love toward others, and facing the future with realistic optimism because Christ reigns and restores those who come to Him in faith.
