## Summary

*Personal Significance!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that true self-worth cannot be securely built on human praise, material possessions, physical beauty, popularity, performance, or competition, because all of these are changing and unreliable. The booklet asserts that lasting dignity comes from belonging to God, being created in His image, being loved by Him, and being redeemed through Christ. Ron Christian contends that believers can experience humility without humiliation and dignity without pride when they recognize that Christ alone is the Master, Message, and Bridegroom, while they serve as His messengers, disciples, and friends. The work also proposes that personal significance grows through surrender, service, a clear conscience, gratitude for God’s blessings, trust in God’s sovereignty, and confidence that God can transform tragedy into triumph. Its central message is that a person’s deepest value is not earned through self-assertion or worldly success, but received through God’s creative purpose, redeeming love, and eternal promises.
