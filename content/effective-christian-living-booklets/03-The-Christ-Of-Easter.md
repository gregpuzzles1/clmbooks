## Summary

*The Christ of Easter!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that the resurrection of Jesus Christ is the foundation of Christian hope, salvation, moral transformation, and confidence in God’s justice. The booklet asserts that Christ’s resurrection vindicated His righteousness, proved His divine claims, demonstrated God’s power, sealed Satan’s defeat, and guaranteed future resurrection life for believers. Ron Christian contends that faith in the resurrection is not merely mental agreement, but a personal commitment to the living Christ, whose risen life brings forgiveness, new birth, strength, and victory over sin and death. The work also proposes that Christianity is a reasonable faith grounded in the historical reality of the resurrection, while still calling for a personal response of trust. Its central message is that the same power that raised Christ from the dead is available today to transform human nature, sustain weak believers, vindicate righteousness, and give lasting hope in a broken world.
