## Summary

*God's Plan of Salvation!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that salvation is God’s gracious provision through the life, death, resurrection, and present reign of Jesus Christ, not the result of human effort, religious performance, morality, or good works. The booklet asserts that humanity is fallen, guilty, alienated from God, and unable to save itself, but that Christ’s atoning death has made reconciliation possible for all who personally respond in repentance, faith, surrender, and trust. Ron Christian contends that faith places the sinner in right standing with God, brings forgiveness, resolves guilt, cleanses the polluted heart, breaks sin’s power, and opens the way to eternal life. The work also proposes that good works do not earn salvation, but naturally flow from saving faith as expressions of love, obedience, gratitude, and service to Christ. Its central message is that God’s plan of salvation restores lost humanity through grace, offers new life in Christ, frees believers from sin’s bondage, and gives assurance, peace, and hope to all who receive the Savior by faith.
