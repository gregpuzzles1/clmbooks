**PDF Title:** *Cultivating Christ Like Character!*

**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Cultivating Christ Like Character!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that Christlike character is formed through the Holy Spirit’s gift of divine love, humble surrender, disciplined thinking, joyful obedience, and practical service to others. The booklet asserts that agape love is the capstone of Christian virtue, enabling believers to love enemies, forgive offenders, serve the needy, show kindness to sinners, and become channels of holy love in a harsh world. Ron Christian contends that humility does not require humiliation, because believers find dignity in belonging to Christ while refusing pride, self-centeredness, and boasting. The work also proposes that Christians must cultivate self-control by fixing their minds on Christ, practice obedience without legalism, become peacemakers, respond to moral evil with balanced righteousness and mercy, and remain patient with unbelieving persons. Its central message is that genuine Christian character is not produced by human effort alone, but by the Holy Spirit’s cleansing and empowering love, which creates inward purity and outward power for witness, compassion, peace, and social responsibility.
