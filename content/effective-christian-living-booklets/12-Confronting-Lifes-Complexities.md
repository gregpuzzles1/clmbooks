## Summary

*Confronting Life's Complexities!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that life’s deepest questions and perplexities can only be faced rightly through faith in Christ, trust in God’s love, and surrender to God’s moral order. The booklet asserts that the roads of materialism, sensual pleasure, worldly fame, and worldly knowledge ultimately lead to emptiness, while the way of Christ leads to meaning, joy, holiness, and eternal hope. Ron Christian contends that human freedom is both frightening and wonderful because each person can choose obedience or rebellion, and that the conscience points to a real moral universe governed by God. The work also proposes that God is incomprehensible yet knowable through Christ, holy in wrath against sin yet loving in mercy toward repentant sinners, and prepared to save all who personally receive Christ’s grace. Its central message is that God’s love, sovereignty, justice, mercy, and indwelling power provide the believer with wisdom, assurance, endurance, and spiritual fruitfulness amid life’s trials, doubts, choices, and unanswered questions.
