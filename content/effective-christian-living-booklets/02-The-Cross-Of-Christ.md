**PDF Title:** *The Cross of Christ!*

**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*The Cross of Christ!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that the cross is the central answer to humanity’s sin, guilt, and separation from God. The booklet asserts that people cannot earn, purchase, or deserve salvation, but must receive God’s redeeming grace through the shed blood of Jesus Christ. Ron Christian contends that Christ’s death was necessary because God is both perfectly just and perfectly loving: sin must be judged, yet sinners may be forgiven because Christ took their penalty through His substitutionary death. The work emphasizes that the cross demonstrates God’s suffering love, satisfies divine justice, provides reconciliation with God and others, and leaves every person empty-handed before grace. Its central message is that salvation rests entirely on the cross of Christ, where God’s justice and love meet, and where sinners find forgiveness, peace, eternal life, and transforming power.
