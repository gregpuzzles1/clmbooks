**PDF Title:** *Heaven Our Eternal Home!*

**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Heaven Our Eternal Home!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that heaven is the believer’s prepared eternal home, a place of holiness, beauty, unity, joy, fellowship, worship, learning, service, and complete freedom from sin, sorrow, sickness, pain, aging, separation, and death. The booklet asserts that Christ’s resurrection is foundational to Christian hope because it guarantees future resurrection life, the fulfillment of God-given aspirations, and the believer’s reception of a new, deathless, recognizable body. Ron Christian contends that death need not be feared by those who trust Christ, because it is not the end of life but the doorway into the nearer presence of God. The work also proposes that God desires all people to be saved, that heaven is spacious enough for all who respond sincerely to God’s light, and that faithful service to Christ will be rewarded by God in time and eternity. Its central message is that believers can live with joy, confidence, holiness, and hope because Christ has prepared a real eternal home where God’s people will know, serve, worship, grow, and rejoice forever.
