## Summary

*Turning Over the Title Deed to Christ* is a devotional reflection in which Ron Christian maintains that full surrender to Christ means giving Him true ownership over every area of the believer’s life. The writing asserts that entire sanctification is not the mechanical removal of an inner substance called sin, but the Holy Spirit’s cleansing and reordering of the heart, mind, desires, attitudes, habits, and relationships. Ron Christian contends that when a believer turns over the “title deed” to Christ, Christ has the right to enter every room of life, including one’s thoughts, emotions, recreation, appetites, loyalties, ambitions, possessions, and relationships. The reflection also proposes that the surrendered life must continue through daily trust, obedience, humility, confession, correction, and renewed consecration. Its central message is that the sanctified Christian life is a life of love: supreme love for God, sincere love for others, and joyful service under the full ownership and cleansing authority of Christ.
