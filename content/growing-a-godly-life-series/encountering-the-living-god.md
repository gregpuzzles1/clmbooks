**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Encountering the Living God* is Book One in the *Growing a Godly Life* devotional series, compiled by Ron Christian, and focuses on the Christian doctrine and experience of salvation. Ron Christian presents salvation as beginning with a spiritual awakening in which the Holy Spirit confronts a person with the reality of sin, personal need, and God’s gracious offer of forgiveness through Jesus Christ. The booklet maintains that genuine conversion involves more than self-improvement, religious formality, or regret; it calls for repentance, faith, surrender, and a transformed life centered on Christ. Through devotional readings, Scripture passages, prayers, affirmations, illustrations, and discussion questions, the work proposes that God lovingly restores broken lives, gives new beginnings, and leads believers into a continuing life of spiritual growth. Its central message is that every person must personally encounter the living God, receive the gift of salvation, and then live out that new life with gratitude, obedience, and trust.
