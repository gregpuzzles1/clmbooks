## Summary

*Practicing the Means of Grace* is Book 2 in the *Growing a Godly Life* devotional series by Ron Christian. The booklet maintains that becoming a Christian may happen through sincere faith and prayer, but growing into a mature believer requires continued participation in the “means of grace” God provides. Ron Christian presents the local church, corporate fellowship, biblical teaching, Scripture, prayer, communion, baptism, worship, hymns, and personal spiritual disciplines as vital channels through which believers are strengthened and shaped. The work contends that Christians are not meant to live isolated spiritual lives, but to grow in the family of God through regular worship, fellowship, reverence, shared devotion, and service. It also emphasizes the Bible as God’s blueprint for life and calls believers to hunger for Scripture, obey it carefully, and allow it to saturate the heart and mind. Through devotional reflections, prayers, affirmations, and discussion questions, the booklet proposes a practical path for deepening communion with God and maturing in the Christian faith.
