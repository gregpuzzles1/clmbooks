**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Refocusing On Life's Priorities* is a Christian devotional work by Ron Christian that calls believers to place eternal values above material possessions, worldly success, personal comfort, and the pursuit of status. The book maintains that materialism can quietly starve the soul by causing people to value temporal things more than spiritual things, and it contends that a Christian's priorities should be shaped by Christ, generosity, gratitude, contentment, simplicity, and service. Ron Christian warns against allowing money, possessions, ambition, and cultural definitions of success to become controlling forces, while also proposing a life marked by thankfulness, wise stewardship, family faithfulness, and concern for people above things. The work also addresses related themes such as remembering one's legacy, passing values to future generations, winning spiritual battles, resisting a corrupt culture, practicing contentment, avoiding self-indulgence, and choosing the good, the better, and the best. Its central message is that Christian maturity requires a continual refocusing of the heart away from temporary treasures and toward God's kingdom, lasting values, and faithful obedience in everyday life.
