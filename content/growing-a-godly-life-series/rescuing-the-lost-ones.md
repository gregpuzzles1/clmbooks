## Summary

*Rescuing The Lost Ones* is a Christian devotional work by Ron Christian that focuses on the believer’s calling to witness, evangelize, and participate in Christ’s Great Commission. The book maintains that sharing the Gospel is not merely a task for pastors or church leaders, but a responsibility given to every follower of Christ. Ron Christian emphasizes preparation for witnessing through repentance, prayer, humility, Scripture, and dependence on the Holy Spirit. He contends that effective witness must combine clear biblical truth with compassion, love, boldness, tolerance, and sensitivity toward people who are lost, hurting, or resistant to faith. The work also proposes that Christians must regain a clear vision for souls, seeing every person as loved by God and full of potential for transformation in Christ. Through devotional reflections, Bible passages, questions, prayers, and affirmations, the book calls believers to overcome fear, guilty silence, spiritual dullness, and self-focus so they can lovingly rescue the perishing, care for the dying, and point others to the saving grace of Jesus Christ.
