## Summary

*Practicing The Presence Of God* is a Christian devotional work in the *Growing A Godly Life* series that focuses on living in continual awareness of God's nearness, majesty, love, and personal care. Ron Christian maintains that Scripture presents God as both high and holy, yet also deeply personal and intimately involved in the lives of His people. The book contends that believers are called not merely to perform Christian duties, but to commune with the Lord moment by moment, allowing worship, prayer, praise, service, and daily decisions to flow from a living relationship with Him. Ron Christian emphasizes that the sacred and the ordinary are not separate for the Christian; God's presence may be recognized in nature, work, relationships, household tasks, suffering, guidance, worship, and unexpected acts of care. The devotional readings invite readers to sit at Jesus' feet, hear God's voice, trust His shepherding protection, receive courage and peace, and let service become the overflow of love for God. Its central message is that a godly life is sustained by practicing the presence of God in every circumstance and learning to see all of life as an opportunity for divine communion.
