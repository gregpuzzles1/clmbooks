## WANT TO ENJOY THE BENEFITS OF MANY CHRISTIAN BOOKS - FREE TO YOU?

Since April 1995, "CHRISTIAN LIVING MINISTRIES" (founded by Ron Christian) has been distributing dozens of Christian books, freely, to many persons who have profited from these devotional and inspirational insights, all based on God's Word (Bible), and all focused in one way or another on the Blessed Son of God (Jesus Christ)!

## You now, as a "visitor"

to this special Web Site, can own any or all of these prayerfully-written books! You can "download" any book that you believe would be of interest to you personally (or to your friends and family). Most of these books were carefully and thoughtfully written, over a period of several years by Ron Christian (who served as a long-time Pastor of an Evangelical Church in Fort Collins, Colorado). The writings are interesting, clear, and relevant to the topics you have chosen.

## These books are mainly devotionally-oriented,

and can be used in several ways: (1) For one's own personal "Quiet Times", alone with God; (2) For material that could be incorporated in a minister's teaching or preaching; (3) Especially for small group study by adults or by teens, midweek or for Sunday School classes. The individual chapters in all the books contain enough content, and yet are short enough in length, that they could serve well as material for small groups within the local Church. There are thought-provoking questions listed at the end of almost all chapters, to provide class participants an opportunity to review and to discuss the main content of each chapter; (4) because there is a developing theme throughout the books, these books can be used and read like any other "regular" book.

## There are more than 30 books that are offered to you

WITHOUT COST TO YOU, other than the small cost of paper that you use to print the ebooks you "Download"; the book or books that you think you would enjoy as a tool for your own spiritual growth or the growth of your family or small group in your local Church.
