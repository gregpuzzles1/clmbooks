**PDF Title:** *The Spirit Filled Life!*

**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*The Spirit Filled Life!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that the Christian life must be lived through the cleansing, empowering, and indwelling work of the Holy Spirit. The booklet asserts that believers need more than forgiveness for sinful actions; they also need inner cleansing from pride, selfishness, impurity, deceit, and self-centeredness. Ron Christian contends that Christianity cannot be lived merely by human effort, moral discipline, or imitation of Christ, but through Christ’s life and power working within the believer. The work proposes that self-surrender to God is not self-loss, but the path to true freedom, self-realization, inward integrity, outward holiness, meaningful service, and mastery over the inner self. Its central message is that the Spirit-filled life is a Christ-centered life of purity, humility, good works, God-like love, and joyful obedience made possible by the Holy Spirit.
