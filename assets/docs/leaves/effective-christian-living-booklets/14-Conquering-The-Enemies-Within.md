**PDF Title:** *Conquering The Enemies Within!*

**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Conquering The Enemies Within!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that the believer’s inner struggles can be brought under Christ’s cleansing, healing, and transforming power. The booklet asserts that money, anger, doubt, stress, criticism, failure, ingratitude, depression, fear, inferiority, insecurity, jealousy, envy, covetousness, and guilt can become destructive when they are ruled by self-centeredness rather than surrendered to God. Ron Christian contends that anger can be rechanneled toward righteous purposes, honest doubt can lead to reasoned faith, and surrender to Christ frees believers from self-worship, peer comparison, bitterness, and spiritual bondage. The work also proposes practical spiritual responses such as confession, forgiveness, gratitude, prayer, faith, repentance, trust in God’s promises, and renewed dependence on the Holy Spirit. Its central message is that Christians are not helpless before their inner enemies, because Christ provides forgiveness for real guilt, freedom from false guilt, peace amid burdens, courage against fear, and grace to grow into a Christlike character.
