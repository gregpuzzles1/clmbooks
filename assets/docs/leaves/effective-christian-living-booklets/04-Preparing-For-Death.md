**PDF Title:** *Preparing For Death!*

**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Preparing For Death!* is an Effective Christian Living booklet in which Ron Christian maintains that believers can face death without terror because Christ has conquered death and the Good Shepherd will remain with His people through the valley of death. The booklet asserts that confessed sins are forgiven and will not be remembered against the believer in eternity, because Christ has already borne sin’s judgment through His death on the cross. Ron Christian contends that God is both merciful Savior and righteous Judge, calling readers to confession, repentance, faith, surrender, restitution, obedience, and lifelong trust in Christ. The work also proposes that preparing for death is not merely a final-moment concern, but a daily way of living close to God, fixing one’s attention on Christ, resisting worldliness, and maintaining faith through grace. Its central message is that the person who is ready to die through faith in Christ is also ready to live with confidence, forgiveness, spiritual security, and hope of heaven.
