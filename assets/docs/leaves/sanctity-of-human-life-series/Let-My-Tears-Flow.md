# Let My Tears Flow

**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Let My Tears Flow* is a Christian devotional book about sorrow, grief, repentance, suffering, and the comfort God gives in the middle of life’s painful experiences. Ron G. Christian reflects on personal and family stories, including the care of his elderly mother, the losses experienced by loved ones, depression, loneliness, death, illness, accidents, and other deep sorrows. The book emphasizes that tears are not a sign of weak faith; rather, they can be part of honest Christian living when brought before God in trust. Christian presents Jesus as the compassionate Savior who understands human heartbreak, weeps with those who suffer, calls sinners to repentance, and offers grace, forgiveness, healing, and eternal hope. Throughout the book, suffering is treated as real and painful, but not meaningless, because God is portrayed as the great Comforter and Compensator who can bring good, growth, and final restoration even from life’s darkest experiences.
