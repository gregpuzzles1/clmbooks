# Reaching Out In Holy Love

**PDF Title:** *Reaching Out In Holy Love*  
**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Reaching Out In Holy Love* is a Christian devotional work by Ron Christian that maintains that genuine Christian faith must be expressed through visible love, humble service, compassionate communication, and practical care for others. The book asserts that good works do not replace faith, but they are the natural fruit of repentance, obedience, and love for Christ. Ron Christian emphasizes that believers are called to participate in the Great Commission not only through preaching or formal ministry, but also through listening, encouraging, nurturing friendships, welcoming new believers, caring for neighbors, including singles and overlooked people, and building bridges across personal, cultural, and religious differences. The work proposes that Christlike service requires humility rather than competition, willingness rather than impressive ability, and a readiness to serve even when the work is unnoticed. Through devotional reflections, biblical examples, prayers, affirmations, and discussion questions, the book calls Christians to become people of holy love whose words, actions, relationships, and everyday service help reveal the compassion of Christ to a hurting world.
