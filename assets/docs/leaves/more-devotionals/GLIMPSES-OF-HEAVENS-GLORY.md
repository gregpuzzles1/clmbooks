**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Glimpses Into Reality* is a Christian devotional work by Ron Christian that reflects on heaven, hell, death, salvation, judgment, compassion, and the wideness of God’s mercy. Ron Christian maintains that God desires every person to be saved, that Christ’s atoning death provides salvation for all, and that the Holy Spirit works through conscience, truth, compassion, and prevenient grace to draw people toward God. The book contends that heaven is a prepared home of fellowship, joy, recognition, holiness, service, and eternal growth in the presence of Christ, while hell is a tragic destiny never intended for human beings but connected to persistent rejection of God’s love. Christian also asserts that God judges people according to the light they have received and the heart response they make to that light, including lives marked by genuine compassion. Throughout the work, he proposes that believers should live with hope, humility, evangelistic concern, and deep confidence in the mercy and justice of God. Its central message is that reality is ultimately centered on the loving God who seeks to save, transform, comfort, and bring redeemed persons into eternal life with Him.
