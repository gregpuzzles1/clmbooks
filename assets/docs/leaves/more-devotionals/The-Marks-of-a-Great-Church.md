**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*The Marks of a Great Church* is an encouraging and structured Christian work by Ron Christian that outlines the spiritual and practical characteristics defining an effective local congregation. The book maintains that a vital community of faith goes beyond superficial commitments, emphasizing the absolute holiness of God's day and addressing common secular excuses for neglecting communal worship. Christian contends that true church health is anchored in deep structural unity amid personal diversity, dedicated study of the Scriptures, and a lifestyle of unceasing prayer and cheerful, sacrificial giving. Furthermore, the text explores the practical intersection of faith and daily living, proposing that dynamic home dynamics and meaningful worship are vital to fulfilling a congregation's higher mission. Ultimately, the work asserts that a faithful church is marked by abundant good works, heartfelt praise, and a collective obedience that unlocks divine blessings for the community.
