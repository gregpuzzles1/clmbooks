**PDF Title:** *The Super "Natural-Natural" Life!*

**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*The Super "Natural-Natural" Life!* is a Christian devotional and teaching work by Ron Christian that presents the Sermon on the Mount as a portrait of the Spirit-filled life rather than a merely human moral code. The book contends that Jesus' teachings in Matthew 5-7 reveal God's true standard for normal Christian living: humility, purity, mercy, forgiveness, prayer, peacemaking, disciplined discipleship, and love for enemies. Ron Christian maintains that these commands cannot be fulfilled by self-effort alone, because fallen human nature lacks the power to live with Christlike motives and actions. Instead, he emphasizes conversion, repentance, faith, surrender, cleansing, and the indwelling power of the Holy Spirit as the source of a transformed life. The work contrasts the world's natural responses of pride, resentment, revenge, lust, and self-centeredness with the believer's new natural life of holy love, forgiveness, meekness, generosity, and obedience. It also draws from Jesus' parables, the Beatitudes, the Book of James, and practical examples to show how Christian character is formed in daily relationships, trials, temptations, prayer, judgment, possessions, and service. Its central message is that the life Jesus describes becomes genuinely natural only when it is supernaturally empowered by God's grace.
