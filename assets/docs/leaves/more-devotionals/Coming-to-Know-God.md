**PDF Title:** *Coming to Know God*

**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*Coming to Know God* is an in-depth Christian devotional study by Ron Christian that presents God’s plan of salvation through both topical teaching and an exposition of Romans 1–6. The book maintains that every person must respond personally to Christ, moving beyond indifference, delay, outward religion, or shallow interest into genuine repentance, saving faith, and surrendered discipleship. Christian explains humanity’s central problem as sin, guilt, alienation from God, and the inability to save oneself, while emphasizing that salvation is God’s gracious gift through Christ rather than the result of human merit or religious performance. The work also explores justification, regeneration, reconciliation, spiritual growth, faith in the Father, Christian hope, and the believer’s calling to become an instrument for God. Its central message is that coming to know God means receiving Christ, trusting God’s provision, growing in holiness, and living as a transformed witness of God’s saving grace.
