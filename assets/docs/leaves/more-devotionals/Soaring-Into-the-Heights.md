**PDF Title:** *Soaring Into the Heights!*

**Author:** Ron G. Christian

## Summary

*Soaring Into the Heights!* is a Christian devotional and teaching work by Ron Christian that presents the Christian life as a journey upward into salvation, freedom, and spiritual maturity. The book begins by explaining humanity's need for salvation, emphasizing sin, guilt, alienation, repentance, faith, grace, justification, regeneration, Christ's substitutionary sacrifice, redemption, forgiveness, deliverance from fear, personal significance, and restoration for the fallen. Ron Christian maintains that salvation cannot be earned through self-effort or religious performance, but must be received through trusting Christ and responding to God's grace. The second part turns toward spiritual growth, focusing on the Holy Spirit's ministry, Pentecost, healthful Christian living, unity, humility, happiness, contentment, joy, and the balanced Christian life. Throughout the book, Christian calls believers to move beyond guilt and self-reliance into a life shaped by grace, Spirit-filled love, obedience, service, joy, and confidence in God's ability to forgive, restore, and transform.
