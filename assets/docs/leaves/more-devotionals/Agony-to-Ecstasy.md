**PDF Title:** *Agony to Ecstasy!*

**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

Agony to Ecstasy! is a Christian devotional and theological study by Ron Christian that traces humanity's movement from the agony of sin and the Fall to the ecstasy of restored life in Christ. The work maintains that mankind was created in God's image for greatness, fellowship with God, meaningful work, moral freedom, and loving relationship, but lost that greatness through Adam's disobedience and the entrance of sin into the world. Christian contends that the Fall brought suffering, moral depravity, death, alienation from God, and brokenness into human experience, while also showing the need for divine grace and redemption. Drawing heavily from Genesis 1-3 and Romans 5:12-21, the book contrasts Adam, through whom sin and death entered the human race, with Christ, the second Adam, through whom righteousness, grace, abundant life, and eternal hope are offered. Christian emphasizes that human beings cannot regain their lost greatness by self-effort, moral pride, or independence from God, but only by repentance, faith, surrender, and receiving Christ's righteousness. The central message is that the agony caused by sin can be replaced by the ecstasy Christ brings, because where sin abounded, grace abounded much more.
