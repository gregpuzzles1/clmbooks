**Author:** Ron Christian

## Summary

*God For All Seasons* is a devotional and sermon-style collection by Ron Christian that presents the Christian calendar and key life occasions as opportunities to recognize God's presence, grace, and call to discipleship. The book maintains that God is present in every season of life and that holidays such as Christmas, Easter, Pentecost, Thanksgiving, Mother's Day, Father's Day, Independence Day, and other special observances can deepen worship, gratitude, compassion, prayer, stewardship, mission, and Christian service. Christian emphasizes the incarnation, the cross, the resurrection, the sanctity of human life, the personality of love, and the believer's responsibility to live with humility, generosity, courage, and obedience. The work also addresses practical and pastoral themes such as inventorying one's life, honoring family, caring for children, remembering veterans, evangelizing others, and finding hope in times of grief and death. Its central message proposes that every season, celebration, and solemn occasion should direct believers toward Christ, shape holy character, and inspire active love toward God and neighbor.
